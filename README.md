# Treasury — Feedback & Support

Welcome! 👋 This repository is the central place to **report issues, ask questions, and share suggestions** about the Treasury system.

There is **no code here** — this repo exists only to collect and track your feedback. Our team monitors it regularly and will respond directly in each issue.

🔗 **Treasury platform:** [treasury.surge.sh](http://treasury.surge.sh/)

---

## What this repository is for

Use this space whenever you want to:

- 🐞 **Report a bug** — something isn't working as expected
- ❓ **Ask a question** — you're unsure how a feature works or how to do something
- 💡 **Suggest an improvement** — you have an idea to make the system better
- 📄 **Request documentation** — something is unclear or missing in the docs

If you're not sure which category fits, don't worry — just open an issue and we'll help you sort it out.

---

## How to open an issue

1. Go to the **[Issues](../../issues)** tab at the top of this repository.
2. Click the green **New issue** button.
3. Give your issue a **clear, short title** that summarizes the topic.
4. Fill in the description with as much detail as you can (see tips below).
5. Click **Submit new issue**.

That's it! You'll get a notification whenever someone replies.

> **Don't have a GitHub account?** Creating one is free at [github.com/signup](https://github.com/signup). You need an account to open and comment on issues.

---

## Writing a helpful issue

The more context you give, the faster we can help. When relevant, please include:

**For bugs 🐞**
- What you were trying to do
- What you expected to happen
- What actually happened
- Steps to reproduce it (1, 2, 3…)
- Screenshots or screen recordings, if possible
- When it happened (date/time) and which environment (e.g. production, staging)

**For questions ❓**
- What you're trying to accomplish
- What you've already tried or looked at
- The specific part of the system you have a question about

**For suggestions 💡**
- The problem or limitation you're facing today
- Your proposed idea or improvement
- Why it would be valuable

---

## Before you open an issue

- 🔍 **Search existing issues** first — your question may already have been answered. Use the search bar in the [Issues](../../issues) tab.
- 👍 If you find an existing issue that matches yours, add a **thumbs-up reaction** or a comment instead of opening a duplicate. This helps us prioritize.

---

## What happens after you submit

1. We review new issues regularly.
2. We may ask follow-up questions in the comments — please keep an eye on notifications.
3. We'll label and prioritize the issue, and let you know the next steps.
4. Once resolved, the issue will be **closed** with a short explanation.

---

## ⚠️ Please do not share sensitive information

Issues in this repository may be visible to others. **Never post** passwords, private keys, secrets, personal data, or confidential business information. If you need to share something sensitive, mention it in the issue and we'll arrange a secure channel.
